Help

This help is for users who want to manipulate (Add, Edit and Delete) the questions showing on the location-based quiz. The users who want to use the quiz app click dowload below.

In order to go back to the setting app click 'Back to App' at the bottom of this page.

Download the quiz application

Add Question

Step 1

Click Add button near the list of questions

The question form will be loaded below the list. You might need to scroll/drag down down to see the form


Step 2

Fill in the question. The image URL of the question is optional.

Note that: the image will be shown as a part of the question in the quiz app.


Step 3

Fill in choices and select the correct answer. These choices' image URL are optional.

Note that: the images will be shown as a part of each choice in the quiz app.


Step 4

Locate the question position. Then click 'Start Data Upload'.

You have choices to drag the marker on the map, or click locate me button, or fill in the coordinates


Step 5

Locate the question position. Then click 'Start Data Upload'.

You have choices to drag the marker on the map, or click the locate me button, or fill in the coordinates


Step 6

If the question is uploaded succesfully, there will be a message as shown below. Click OK to continue.

Then, your recent question will be shown on the list.


Error Handling

Missing information

You might miss to specify any choices or question. So, fill in the missing part.



You might miss to select the correct answer. So, check the correct answer box.


Taken location

The selected location is already taken by the another question. So, move to another location

Edit Question

Step 1

Click edit button at the question on the list.

You might need to reload the list by clicking at the reload button at the end of the page.


Step 2

The form of the selected question will be shown just below the list. Fill in the form same as adding question and click 'Edit Question' button.

Note that: You might need to scroll/drag down down to see the form.


Step 3

If the question is updated successfully, there is a message shown as below. Click OK to continue.


Delete Question

Step 1

Click delete button at the question on the list.


Step 2

Click OK to confirm.


Step 3

If the question is deleted successfully, there is a message shown as below. Click OK to continue. Then the question will be removed from the list
